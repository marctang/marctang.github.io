Title: The role of cognition for unpredictable content in the evolution of writing.

This project (2026-2029) is hosted at the Institut Jean Nicod and the Museum of National History in Paris and hypothesises that contextually unpredictable content is more likely to be encoded.

The webpage of the project can be found [here](https://anr.fr/Project-ANR-25-CE28-7755).
