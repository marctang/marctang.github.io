Title: Exploring the frontiers of phylolinguistics.

This project (2024-2027) is hosted at the CRLAO, the CEREMADE and the Museum of National History in Paris and explores the possibilities and push the frontiers of computational language phylogenies.

The webpage of the project can be found [here](https://anr.fr/Project-ANR-24-CE38-0559).
