Role: Principal Investigator

Title: The role of linguistic and non-linguistic factors in the evolution of nominal classification systems.

This project (2021-2024) is hosted at the DDL (Dynamics of Language) lab in Lyon and aims at building a database on nominal classification systems to identify the factors affecting their evolution.

The webpage of the project can be found [here](https://anr.fr/Project-ANR-20-CE27-0021).
